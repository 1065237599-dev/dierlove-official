Frequently Asked Questions.

What is your typical sample lead time? ↓

Our standard physical sampling lead time is 7-10 business days, depending on the availability of custom fabrics or accessories.

Can you help with technical design and pattern making? ↓

Yes, our in-house R&D team includes expert pattern makers who can turn your rough sketches or inspiration photos into technical spec sheets and production-ready patterns.

Do you ship globally and handle customs? ↓

Dierlove has extensive experience in global logistics. We offer door-to-door (DDP) or port-to-port (FOB) shipping to over 100 countries, including handling all export documentation.

Is your factory BSCI or Oeko-Tex certified? ↓

Yes, we adhere to international labor and environmental standards. We are happy to provide our latest audit reports and fabric certificates upon request.
