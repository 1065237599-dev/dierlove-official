The 12-Step QC Protocol

Quality is never an accident. Every Dierlove garment passes through twelve independent verification points before it is approved for shipment.

01

Fabric Inspection

Raw materials are inspected for color consistency, tension, and flaws using professional grading machines.

02

Shrinkage Test

Sample strips from every roll undergo standardized washing to ensure 0-1% shrinkage in final production.

03

CAD Patterning

Patterns are digitized for 1:1 precision, optimizing fabric yield and ensuring fit consistency across sizes.

04

Precision Cutting

Automated cutting systems provide millimetric accuracy, critical for complex lingerie and swimwear components.

05

First-Piece Approval

The first garment off each line is verified against the tech pack by the senior QC manager before mass assembly.

06

In-Line Inspection

QC inspectors monitor each production line hourly to identify and rectify sewing issues in real-time.

07

Thread Clearing

Every garment is manually cleared of loose threads, ensuring a clean, high-end finish for premium retail.

08

Symmetry Check

Critical for bras and swimwear: left-to-right symmetry and alignment are checked on specialized mannequins.

And 4 more stages including Ironing, Label Auditing, Metal Detection, and AQL Final Audit.
